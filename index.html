Peanut butter and Jelly Milkshake

Good Milkshake from Delish.com

image from delish made by Suzanne Clements Www.delish.com

Ingredients:
2tbsp of Peanut butter
2tbsp of Jelly
2tbsp of vanilla Ice cream
1/4 cup of milk

Steps:

1. Blend

Put the Ingredients into the blender and blend it.

2. Done... plus options

Serve it, and can use additional Peanut butter and jelly for tasting.
